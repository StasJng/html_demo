THÔNG TIN VỀ FURAMA RESORT DANANG

Property Location
With a stay at Furama Resort Danang in Da Nang (Ngũ Hành Sơn), you'll be within a 5-minute drive of My Khe Beach and Han River. This 5-star resort is 2.8 mi (4.4 km) from Dragon Bridge and 3 mi (4.9 km) from Tran Thi Ly Bridge.

Rooms
Make yourself at home in one of the 198 air-conditioned rooms featuring fireplaces. Rooms have private balconies. Wired and wireless Internet access is complimentary, while DVD players and satellite programming provide entertainment. Conveniences include phones, as well as safes and desks.

Amenities
Pamper yourself with a visit to the spa, which offers massages, body treatments, and facials. After dipping into one of the 2 outdoor swimming pools, you can spend some time at the private beach. This resort also features complimentary wireless Internet access, concierge services, and babysitting/childcare (surcharge). Getting to nearby attractions is a breeze with the area shuttle (surcharge) that operates within 22 kilometers.

Business Amenities
Featured amenities include complimentary wired Internet access, a business center, and limo/town car service. A roundtrip airport shuttle is provided for a surcharge (available 24 hours), and free valet parking is available onsite.

Dining
Enjoy Asian cuisine at Cafe Indochine, one of the resort's 3 restaurants, or stay in and take advantage of the 24-hour room service. Snacks are also available at the 3 coffee shops/cafés. When you are in need of a refreshing drink, visit the swim-up bar or one of 5 bars/lounges, or 2 poolside bars. Buffet breakfasts are available daily from 6:30 AM to 10 AM for a fee.
